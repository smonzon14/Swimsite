What Practice is Like

Practices are Monday Wednesday and Friday in the afternoon (3:00-4:00) and Tuesday and Thursday at night (8:00-9:00). We have a Slow lane for beginners, Intermediate for medium paced swimmers, and Fast for, well, fast swimmers.
